Last Unit

An infinite runner set in a volcanic landscape

Role: Technical Artist Team Size: 15 Engine: Unity Year: 2024

My Contributions

Shaders & Materials

Created a lava material with scrolling emissive flow for the environment's lava pools. I also authored a shader for the lava rocks thrown at the player that uses world-space masking to reveal glowing lava only in the crevices and recesses of the rock mesh, keeping the outer surface looking solid and cooled. Additionally, I built a post-processing speed lines effect that activates during fast movement to reinforce the sense of velocity.

Mesh Blockouts

Produced quick mesh blockouts during early production stages to help the team establish level layouts and gameplay spaces before final art was in place.
