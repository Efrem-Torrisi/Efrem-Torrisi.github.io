Revolt: Gear By Gear

A turn-based mobile puzzle game

Role: Technical Artist Team Size: 13 Engine: Unity Year: 2024

My Contributions

Shaders & Rigs

Built a master material for the game's grid system's tiles that allowed artists to control which texture was displayed on each face of the tile based on its position and context within the level, streamlining the environment art workflow. I also made a fog shader to create a smooth visual transition between the playable gameplay area and the empty void beyond the level boundaries, giving the edges of the map a cleaner, more polished look instead of a hard cutoff. Additionally, I created a subtle full-screen post-processing shader that mimics the look of a security camera feed, with scanlines and slight distortion, to sell the fantasy of the player being watched and reinforce the game's escape from surveillance theme. I also rigged and skinned one of the enemy character models, preparing it for animation.

VFX

Produced various visual effects for gameplay feedback and polish, including crusher activation and impact, enemy and spotlight attacks, player attacks and death sequences.
