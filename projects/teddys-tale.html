Teddy's Tale

A narrative stealth-puzzle game where you play as a teddy bear

Role: Technical Artist Team Size: 8 Engine: Unreal Engine 4 Year: 2022

My Contributions

Materials & Textures

Created all the materials and textures used across the game's environments and props.

Fur Shader

Built a fur shader for the teddy bear main character using parallax occlusion mapping to give the illusion of volumetric fur on a flat surface, adding depth and softness to the character.

Motion Capture Direction

Directed the motion capture sessions for character animations. Capture was done using OptiTrack Motive software, and I cleaned up and retargeted the recorded clips in Autodesk MotionBuilder before importing them into the engine.
