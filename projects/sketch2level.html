Sketch2Level

Sketch-to-Level Design Pipeline

Role: Solo Developer Engine: Unreal Engine 5 Year: 2025

Overview

A rapid prototyping pipeline that converts hand-drawn sketches into playable 3D level geometry. Designers can sketch rough layouts on paper or tablet, and the tool interprets the drawing to generate blockout-quality environments ready for iteration.
